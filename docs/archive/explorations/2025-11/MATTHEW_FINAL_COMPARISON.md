## Complete Bio (All Sources)

Matthew Galley, VP of Product Design at Solo, is a multi-disciplinary product designer, strategist, and developer nicknamed "The Mad Scientist" for his innovative ideas and passion for design.

A lifelong artist who has loved art since childhood—whether digital, print, oil, or chalk—Matthew is now following that dream through product design and development. With 23 years of experience across 11 companies, he brings deep expertise in UX design, design systems, DesignOps, and JavaScript development.

His work spans from building Solo's first design system (Project Blaze) and creating the Adaptive Systematic Experience Builder framework, to developing React applications and leading design team migrations to Figma. He holds UX Design and UX Research Expert certifications from Pluralsight and has won 7 business and entrepreneurship awards.

Based in Madison, WI, Matthew is a wacky, fun-loving husband and father of three who enjoys tabletop games, LEGOs, rally car driving, sports, and exploring the world.
