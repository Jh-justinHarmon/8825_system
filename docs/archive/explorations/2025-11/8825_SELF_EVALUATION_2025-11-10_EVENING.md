## 💡 BIG LEAP RECOMMENDATION

### **Solution: "Ship Week" Protocol**

**Concept:** Dedicate one week per month to ONLY shipping stuck items

**Rules:**
1. **No new explorations** during Ship Week
2. **No infrastructure work** during Ship Week  
3. **Only finishing and shipping** existing "ready" items
4. **Must ship at least 2 items** per Ship Week
5. **Document learnings** after each ship

**Implementation:**

#### **Week 1 of Each Month = Ship Week**

**Pre-Week (Sunday):**
- List all "ready to promote" items
- Pick 2-3 highest value
- Block calendar
- Set success criteria

**Monday-Wednesday: Build**
- Take first item from "ready" to "done"
- No perfectionism
- Ship MVP version
- Document as you go

**Thursday-Friday: Ship & Validate**
- Deploy/activate
- Test with real use
- Gather feedback
- Document learnings

**Weekend: Reflect**
- What worked?
- What didn't?
- Update protocols
- Plan next Ship Week
